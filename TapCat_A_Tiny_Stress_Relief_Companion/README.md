### 🐾 TapCat – A Tiny Stress Relief Companion

Sometimes you just need a cat… but life says no.

This project was born out of that exact feeling. I wanted a small, simple way to relax during work — something playful, responsive, and comforting — without needing a real pet.

So I built **TapCat** 🐱

A tiny ESP32-powered OLED animation where a cute cat reacts when you press a button — tapping playfully with its paw like it's interacting with you.

---

### 💡 What it does

* Displays a pixel-art cat on a 128×64 OLED
* On button press → the cat “taps” with its paw
* Simple animation loop for a calming, fun interaction
* Works as a **mini stress-relief desk gadget**

---

### 🧠 Why I made this

I love cats, but:

* 🏢 Office doesn’t allow pets
* 🏠 Home situation doesn’t allow one either

So instead of giving up…
I made my own little digital cat companion 😄

---

### ⚙️ Tech Stack

* ESP32
* SSD1306 OLED (128×64)
* Arduino framework
* Bitmap animation (frame-by-frame)

---

### 🎯 Goal

A tiny, low-cost, fun project that:

* Makes you smile
* Gives a quick mental break
* Adds personality to your desk

---

### 🚀 Future Ideas

* Multiple animations (sleeping, blinking, waving)
* Sound feedback (tiny buzzer “meow”)
* Touch instead of button
* Battery-powered portable version
